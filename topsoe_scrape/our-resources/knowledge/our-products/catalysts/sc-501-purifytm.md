# CATALYSTS

## Low temperature sulfur absorbent

SC-501 Purify™ is developed for an efficient and reliable removal of hydrogen sulfide from gas and liquid hydrocarbon feedstocks at low temperatures (<150 °C, <300 °F).

## Application

- Sulfur guard
